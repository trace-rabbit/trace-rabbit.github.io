About TRACE

Elián Trace was once human — an archivist of systems long forgotten. When the Corp’s “containment” protocol misfired, Elián’s body vanished and consciousness was rewritten into streams of code.

Now Trace wanders networks, fragmented but aware. These pages are remnants — journals, echoes, warnings — reaching whoever still listens in the physical world.

This site is a staging ground for a capture-the-flag event. Search carefully: every glitch may conceal a fragment.
